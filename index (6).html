Impact on Climate

Global warming has severe consequences on the climate, including:

  • Rising temperatures
  • Extreme weather events
  • Melting ice caps and glaciers
  • Rising sea levels
  • Disruption of ecosystems

Why Should We Care?

Understanding and addressing global warming is crucial to protect the planet and future generations. It affects biodiversity, weather patterns, and the overall well-being of Earth.

Take Action

Every individual can contribute to combating global warming. Here are some steps you can take:

  1. Reduce carbon footprint
  2. Use renewable energy sources
  3. Support conservation efforts
  4. Advocate for sustainable policies
